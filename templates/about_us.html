about us

Our story

Torio was born in 1992 and began its journey as a retail brand specializing in high end children wear within the Indonesian market. Our international collections, inspired by the European’s fashionable design spread far and wide, and by 1996 have been featured in more than 7 independent Torio show rooms and in multiple department stores in Jakarta.

Our Partnership

To focus better on the ever increasing demand in the global market and with Torio’s new partnership with Walt Disney Company, Torio in 2007 briefly ends its expansion in Indonesia and swift its full aim to focus on the international market.
